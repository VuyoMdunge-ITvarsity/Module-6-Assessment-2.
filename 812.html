The 812 Competitizione is the epitome of Ferrari's traditional front engine cars.

The Ferrari 812 was already an amazing machine with its 800 horsepower screaming V12, but the Competizione only elevates it more.

The car features 830 horsepower, more carbon fiber, upgraded suspension, and an even louder exhaust making the driving experience even more exhilarating.

Like many limited-production Ferraris, the 812 Competizione is produced in limited numbers, adding to its exclusivity and desirability among enthusiasts and collectors. Only a select few fortunate individuals will have the opportunity to own and experience this extraordinary machine. Overall, the Ferrari 812 Competizione represents the pinnacle of Ferrari's engineering prowess, offering breathtaking performance, cutting-edge technology, and unparalleled driving dynamics for those lucky enough to experience it.
