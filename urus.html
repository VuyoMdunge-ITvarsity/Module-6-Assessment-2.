The Urus perfomante is as close ypu will get to a practical lamborghini.

The Lamborghini Urus Performante is a high-performance version of the standard Urus SUV. It inherits the ethos of Lamborghini's "Performante" designation, which typically signifies enhancements in terms of power, aerodynamics, and handling..

With a revised turbo system and better exhaust the peformante produces 30 more horsepower over the standard urus.

The performante also adopts more carbon fiber and ditches the air suspension for less weight and better handling while feeling more like a standard performance car. It is very impressive for a car of this size and the capability of going offroad with the family.
