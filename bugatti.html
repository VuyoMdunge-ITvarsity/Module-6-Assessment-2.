Say bonjour to the Bugatti W16 Mistral

The Mistral is said to be their fastest and most powerful roadster they havee made ever sinnce the veyron Grand sport Vittesse.

To make this a pure bugatti they have fitted their historic W16 engine and have the chassis out of their strongest carbon fiber to keep weight down and still maintain rigidity.

Bugatti is only making 99 W16-powered Mistrals. Each starts at $5 million. A price which is a clear statement that this car goes straight into the stratosphere of supercars.
