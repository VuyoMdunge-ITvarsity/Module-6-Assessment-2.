The cc850 is the reincarnation the brands very first and is a brilliant homage to it.

It marries a fully manual gearbox and an automatic gearbox making sure you have the option to feel connected to the car and relax on other occasions.

With such a potent powerplant, the CC850 delivers exceptional performance figures. It's capable of accelerating from 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) in just under 3 seconds and has a top speed that exceeds 390 km/h (242 mph).

As with all koenigseggs the car has very few units in the wild with only 70 cars being produced and all going for 2 million dollars, if you ask any koenigsegg owner they would say the price is more than worth it with what you get in return.
